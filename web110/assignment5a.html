Activity Summary

Activity 1: Complete the following tutorial

This activity, interestingly enough, did not include a tutorial; it was an introductory summary page to the concept of social media and various social media sites. The site gave brief service descriptions of a selection of social media sites, with an interesting mix of US-based and UK-based. Some of the US descriptions are out of date: namely, X is referred to as Twitter, and Meta as Facebook. The lack of modernity is also displayed in its omission of several currently popular social media sites, such as Instagram, Reddit, or Tumblr. The activity then went on to ask us to decide which of the following sites were of interest. As someone in Gen Z, I've had an X account since before it became X. It is very easily my favorite, of the multiple listed. Unfortunately, due to how dated this site is, there isn't very much that is particularly actionable in the future just based off the information in this article, but I suppose it counts that I'm currently active on X (formerly Twitter).
